Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, (spooned and leveled)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Toppings

  • butter
  • maple syrup
  • confectioners' sugar
  • honey
  • jams
  • preserves
  • sweetened whipped cream
  • chocolate syrup

Procedure 

        Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat. 
        Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 
        1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges 
        are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on 
        the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
